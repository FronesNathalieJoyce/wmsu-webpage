Congratulations Dr. Ma. Carla A. Ochotorena, our new president!

September 3, 2020

Congratulations Dr. Ma. Carla A. Ochotorena, our new president!

The Board of Regents, the university’s highest decision-making body, elected Dr. Ochotorena as its 6th president in a special meeting held September 2.

A holder of Doctor of Philosophy in Public Administration and Governance degree from the University of the Visayas, Dr. Ochotorena served as WMSU’s Vice President for Academic Affairs (VPAA) from 2014 to 2020. She also served as the Dean of Admissions on concurrent capacity as OIC-VPAA from June 2004 to 2013.

Additional 400 KW Solar Energy to power WMSU Facilities

February 6, 2020

The Western Mindanao State University (WMSU) yet again showed how serious the university is towards being a more eco-friendly and sustainable university. WMSU officially launched the additional 400 Kilowatts (KW) renewable Energy Solar Facility last November 27, 2019 at Dr. Bruno Juanitoa Gymnasium.

Dr. Milabel E. Ho, WMSU President said that the additional 400 KW energy will be very beneficial to the whole WMSU community as it reduces the cost of the monthly payment in electricity and reduces the effects of climate change. Moreover, she emphasized that every one of us has a role in conserving not only in the consumption of electricity but also in the consumption of water.
